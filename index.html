    Doctors

    Dedicated team of sub specialist to cater all eye diseases from corneal pole to retinal pole of the eyeball and taking care of paediatric to geriatric eye services. The center is Govt. recognised center for corneal transplant, also vision center for National Programme for Control of Blindness (NPCB). Apart from having ISO-9001: 2008 and FEQH Accreditation, hospital is also accredited by National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Health Care Providers (NABH). The center is post graduate teaching institute for Diploma in Ophthalmic Medicine and Surgery (DOMS). The centre is recognised under Central Govt. Health Scheme (CGHS). Kenia Foundation has been formed as a social responsibility to fight against blindness.
